    Farley Lai is a senior associate researcher in Machine Learning at the NEC Laboratories America. He received his Ph.D. under the supervision of Prof. Octav Chipara in Computer Science at the University of Iowa. High performance stream processing to model the runtime behavior and improve resource efficiency of stream applications through compile-time analysis serves as his research foundation. His M.S. was completed with thesis on distributed mutual exclusion and scheduling fairness at the National Central University in Taiwan. Before pursuing a Ph.D., he had been working in the surveillance industry and led a project of developing video management software. His current research focuses on defect detection in the manufacturing industry.

    He is reachable via social networks.
